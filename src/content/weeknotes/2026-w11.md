I had a good mix of work and play during my free time this week. atex has been
getting some love with new features, notably a [PLC](https://plc.directory/)
client and inter-service authentication token validation to make way for XRPC
server utilities which I'm working on now. I also did a bunch of refactoring to
the DID document struct to add some sub-structs and decode the signing key
within to make it easier to work with for some use cases. Overall I'm happy with
how I'm going with it, and I'm hoping to have a release out either next week or
the week after once the aforementioned XRPC server utils are done.

Outside of programming, I got PCSX2 installed on my desktop recently and have
been playing through the Ratchet & Clank saga once again. Being able to upscale
them to 12 times the internal resolution is really cool, seeing how crisp it can
get. I also reset my progress for them on RetroAchievements so I can try to 100%
them all on the one machine, mostly to get that dopamine hit of the achievement
notifications again.
